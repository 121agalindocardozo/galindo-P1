About Me

Hello. My name is Alexander Galindo. I am 17 years old, hispanic and bilangual, and in my third year of studying web design at Freehold High School. I really enjoy web desing because it allows me to be as creative as I want when making a website about things that interest me. Even when making informational websites I still have the ability to make it look pretty and intriguing for the person visiting my website while giving them useful information whether its city guide or a concert venue. City Guide

This is an informational website aimed at tourist planning to explore a new city which in this case is Tokyo Japan. This was made in my second year of web design In this website I included upcoming events, hotels the tourist could stay at including prices, and cultural differences and how a tourist could make their way through the city.

Concert Venue

This website was made last year while I was in my second year of the web design program. It has information about a made up concert venue that is supposed to be informational. I made the logo and name by myself and used it on this website to give people information about upcoming concert events.
